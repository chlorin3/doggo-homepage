My hobbies

Life without a hobby is like food without the treats. It is a hobby which makes living interesting. It is a pursuit outside one's regular work. A hobby gives one joy and pleasure; one does not get tired of it. It is a pastime, relaxation and leisure. In this way, while one is doing what they love, they are also making best use of their time.

My hobby is the activity that I can't live without and need to regularly get involved in it.

I enjoy:

  • road trips and camping,
  • swimming,
  • digging holes at the beach or in grandma's garden,
  • chewing bones, skin and meat sticks,
  • eating good food,
  • sleeping on my back under warm blanket,
  • making eye contact with strangers and barking at them,
  • protecting my apartment,
  • staring at cats, chasing them and being annoying to them (that's my favourite),
  • chasing sticks and balls.
